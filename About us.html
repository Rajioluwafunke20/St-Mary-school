Our Humble Begining

School history

St mary school was founded in 1980 by Reverend mother Augustine, A visionary educator and catholic nun. With passion forr providing quality education to children from all walks of life, Rev Mother Mary Augustine established the school with the mission of fostering academic excellence, moral values, and spiritual growth.

Since its humble begining, st mary school has grown into a reputable institution, known for its commitment to providing a well-rounded education that prepares students for success in an ever-changing world. Today, the school continues to uphold rev mother mary augustine vision, inspiring generations of students to become compassionate, thoughtful, and accomplished individuals.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission Statement

At St. Mary School, our mission is to provide a nurturing, holistic educational environment that empowers students to develop academically, spiritually, and personally. We are committed to fostering critical thinking, compassion, and leadership skills while supporting each student's unique potential.

Our vision for the future

Our vision is to be a leading educational institution that cultivates well-rounded individuals who are intellectually curious, morally grounded, and prepared to make positive contributions to society. We aim to create a transformative learning experience that inspires students to become lifelong learners, ethical leaders, and compassionate global citizens. Key Educational Principles: Promote academic excellence Develop strong moral and spiritual values Encourage personal growth and self-discovery Create a supportive and inclusive learning community Prepare students for future challenges and opportunities
