About Us

St. Mary School's commitment to character development is reflected in our values-based education, emphasizing integrity, leadership, and community service. Our consistently high academic achievements and successful alumni network demonstrate our dedication to nurturing both intellectual growth and personal development. We offer a diverse range of extracurricular activities, from robotics clubs to championship sports teams, ensuring every student finds their passion. Our strong parent-teacher collaboration creates a supportive community that celebrates each child's unique potential and achievements. At St. Mary School, we don't just educate; we inspire, nurture, and empower the leaders of tomorrow.

Our mission

At St. Mary School, we are dedicated to nurturing holistic student development through a comprehensive educational approach that balances academic excellence, moral growth, and personal empowerment. Our mission is to provide a transformative learning environment that challenges students intellectually, supports them emotionally, and prepares them for future success. We are committed to fostering critical thinking, compassion, and leadership skills while instilling strong ethical values

Our values

At St. Mary School, we believe in nurturing more than just academic excellence. Our core values shape the foundation of our educational philosophy: 1.Excellence: We encourage students to strive for their personal best in every endeavor, academic and beyond. 2.Integrity: We foster honesty, ethical behavior, and strong moral character in all our students. 3.Respect: Our community values mutual respect, embracing diversity and treating everyone with dignity. 4.Responsibility: We develop responsible citizens who understand their duties toward community and society. 5.Leadership: Through various opportunities, we cultivate leadership skills and initiative in every student. 6.Compassion: We nurture empathy and kindness, encouraging students to care for others and contribute positively to society.At St. Mary School, we are Nurturing holistic student development through academic excellence, spiritual growth, and personal character formation. Fostering a supportive and inclusive learning environment that respects diversity, promotes mutual understanding, and empowers every student to reach their full potential

What we offer

Chemistry

Chemistry at St. Mary School is a crucial science subject where students explore chemical reactions, molecular structures, and laboratory experiments to develop scientific understanding and analytical skills.

physics

Physics at St. Mary School provides students with comprehensive scientific education, focusing on understanding fundamental physical principles and practical scientific experimentation.

Biology

Biology in St. Mary School offers comprehensive scientific education, providing students with in-depth knowledge of living organisms, their structures, functions, growth, evolution, and interactions with the environment.

We offer Mathematics

Mathematics at St. Mary School is a core subject offering comprehensive learning experiences, equipping students with strong mathematical skills and analytical thinking for academic and future professional development.

We offer Computer Studies

Computer Studies at St. Mary School provides comprehensive technological education, teaching students essential programming skills, digital literacy, and innovative computing concepts to prepare them for the digital age.
